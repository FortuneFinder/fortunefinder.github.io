Delete User Data

FortuneFinder collects user data to provide accurate future predictions. However, we understand that some users may want to delete their data. To delete your data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Send an email to privacy@fortunefinder.com from the email address associated with your account.
  2. In the email, please include your full name and the type of data you wish to delete.
  3. Our team will process your request within 30 days.

Please note that some data may be required to be retained for legal or accounting purposes.
